The 2026 Destination

The Classic heads to the high desert

For its fifteenth edition, the Dirty Birdie Summer Classic crosses into central Washington — to one of the most celebrated links destinations in North America, set high above the mighty Columbia River.

Two championship courses from the inventive mind of David McLay Kidd, firm-and-fast fescue turf, a 14-hole short course, sunset putting, and farm-to-table dining — all a few steps from your room. This is a golf trip built for exactly what we do: great golf, great company, and a trophy on the line.

Where We Stay

The Inn at Gamble Sands

Wake to views of the Columbia River and the golf below, grab a coffee, and walk to the first tee. Rated the best golf resort in Washington — and home base for the whole crew.

77 luxury rooms, fire pits under the high-desert sky, and world-class golf a few minutes from the door. Bring the group; do it all again tomorrow.

Food & Drink
